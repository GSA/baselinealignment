How to tie a shoe: step by step instructions

  1. Take both laces,
  2. And thread one lace over the other.
  3. Then pull them tight. They should form a knot in the middle of the shoe.
  4. Make a bunny ear with each loop. (You should be holding the lace between your thumb and your first one or two fingers).
  5. Tie a knot by placing one bunny ear loop over the other, then tuck it behind and thread it through the hole.
  6. Pull tight.
  7. Repeat this step again to double knot your shoe.
  8. Pull tight and you are done!
